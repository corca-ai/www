grow together

AX Day is not an individual task,Paired Collaboration ApproachIt proceeds as follows.

We share bottlenecks we experience in each other's work processes, define problems from our own perspectives, and explore solutions together.
This process naturally leads to not only individual growth but also improvement of the capabilities of the entire team.

Additionally, AX is not necessarily limited to work.
Some teams implemented a visualized music player, while others created a custom metronome that could be used while running.

In this way, AX Day is a time that crosses the boundary between work and daily life and allows for various attempts.

It starts with a small experiment, but the experience will lead to greater possibilities.
