Our new beginning, Corca moving story

2026-02-02Sunyoung Kim (HR Manager)

Corca has moved to a new space.

In the meantime, I've gone through really small and frequent moves.
Starting with shared office rooms for 6 people, rooms for 12 people and up to 30 people.
As the number of team members increased by one or two, the space also grew little by little.
This time for the first timeindependent officehas been moved to . (Wow👏👏👏)

This move is not just a matter of changing locations,
It was also a process of thinking once again about how we work and what kind of time we want to spend.

I'm going to tell the story!

Why did I move?

The previous office was Corca's first start, and it was a precious space where many moments were shared.

However, I gradually began to feel the limitations in capturing the future direction and growth.

So we decided to find a space that could accommodate both the current Corca and the future Corca!

New office, so different

Introducing Corca’s new office!
Based on an overall bright and warm atmosphere, we wanted to create a comfortable space for long stays.

office entranceThe first thing you do when you come in iscorca logoYou can see it.
The logo was placed in front of the entrance so that Corca can be seen naturally at the beginning and end of the day.

The first space to be introduced isoffice spaceIt is.
We organized the place so that we could communicate naturally, while respecting each person's flow and concentration.

I thought the most important thing was the balance of being able to talk right away when necessary and maintaining each person's rhythm when immersion was needed.

I thought we needed a space where we could rest well and have good conversations, rather than a space where we could only work.

Lounge and rest areaHave a quick chat over a cup of coffee,
This is a space where you can cool off by yourself for a while.

The open kitchen is also designed to allow people to sit and chat freely, allowing communication outside of work to continue naturally.

made of transparent glassmeeting roomwas designed so that the space does not feel stuffy.

When there is an external meeting or important discussion, it can be used privately using a curtain, making it a space that can be used flexibly depending on the situation.

A word from the team members

  • “I can concentrate much better than before, and the atmosphere has become more comfortable.”
  • “I think the feeling of working has become new because the space has changed.”
  • “I’m looking forward to what will happen here in the future.”

In this space we

How much more can we learn and how much can we grow in this new space?

This office allows Corca to try more, fail more often, and
I think it is a starting point that can help you become stronger.

As I spend more time in this space, I look forward to Corca’s future even more 🚀
