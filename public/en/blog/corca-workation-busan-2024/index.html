2024 Corca Walk in Busan

2024-09-13Sunyoung Kim (HR Manager)

Corca leaves for Busan!

The Corca team went to Busan on a work trip from August 26th to August 30th!

Workation is a compound word of work and vacation, and is a type of work where you work while taking a break wherever you want. This was my first time going on a work trip, so I was half excited and half worried, but everyone on the team was very satisfied with the results! I was able to go on a happy walk.

Let's take a look at Corca's first walk, from the preparation process to the 5-day, 4-night walk together.

Corca leaves for Busan!

Are you going on a walk?

As this was our first workshop, the expectations of the team members were very high. In order to meet this expectation, we formed and prepared a work TF team. Thanks to the TF team, we were able to have the ‘best work’ and ‘a fruitful work’ by making group tees, making placards, preparing breakfast, a place for company dinners, and things to do. I would like to take this opportunity to thank you once again.

Workspace prepared by the TF team / Corca 2024 SS T-shirt Workspaces and accommodations with high satisfaction 😊 Introducing accommodations and workspaces with high satisfaction during this walkthrough The Holiday Desker Work Center Yeongdo, where you can work while looking at the sea! It was a very healing place just being able to see the sea in front of me while working 👍. The accommodation was comfortable for one person per room. The view of Yeongdodaegyo Bridge from the window was also the best.

Workspace with a view of the sea / comfortable accommodation Alignment day Did you just go to Busan and work? no! Through the alignment day on the first day, I was able to build precious memories with my team members and become closer. Among them, the most impressive one for me personally was the ‘Let’s Praise Corca’ time. It was a meaningful time where we could praise each other and convey stories and gratitude that we couldn’t normally express! It was so touching...

🥹 In addition to this, we also played a Sudoku game where we divided roles and thought about how to generate profit, and we also played Corca Golden Bell, and a prize was given to the last 🎁 person.

Let's praise Corca

Enjoy Busan🌊 There are so many foods that come to mind when I think of Busan! Corcan people, who are serious about taste, explored delicious Busan food every lunch. I was able to have a happy time eating new and delicious lunches with my team members every day.

You can’t come to Busan and not eat fresh seafood and meat, right? The restaurant tour with team members continued in the evening as well. As the number of Corca team members has increased, it has become more difficult to hold a company dinner in Seoul than before, but during this work trip, we had a great time having a company dinner for the first time in a long time and having a birthday party for a team member who had a birthday in August.

😋 There was a side effect of gaining weight after working out, but when traveling, you should eat without worrying about your weight.

Busan, where my mouth was happy

Vivid work reviews from team members

The Corca team members had a great time in Busan. Let’s find out what this work period was like through the review.

It was my first time trying a program called Workation, and I really enjoyed being with my teammates in a new space.

It was refreshing and all the facilities were great. Thank you very much to everyone who worked hard for the work.

I was glad that the pit stop, travel, and unity all went well in Busan!

It was nice to talk about things I normally couldn’t. It gave me an opportunity to reflect on the way I work again.

I had so much fun playing with my teammates. I made a lot of good memories.

The accommodations were nice, the surrounding scenery was nice, there were a lot of good restaurants, and it was nice to play with my teammates, reorganize teamwork, and relax.

Goodbye to Busan. There are many cases where we don't look back at each other when we only look ahead and run, but during the 4 nights and 5 days Busan walk, we were able to look back at each other and gain the strength to run more energetically. It was a walk where I was able to feel the meaning of ‘us’ once again! Please look forward to what event we will have next.
